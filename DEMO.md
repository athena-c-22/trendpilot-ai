# DEMO.md

This document explains how to demonstrate TrendPilot AI.

The goal of the demo is to show how AI agents collaborate to generate a Go-To-Market strategy from a simple idea.

---

# Demo Scenario

Example user input:

"AI productivity app for university students"

---

# Step 1: User Input

The user enters a topic into the web interface.

Example:

```
AI productivity app for university students
```

---

# Step 2: Research Phase

The system activates multiple agents.

Search Agent:

* identifies competitors
* collects market data
* extracts industry trends

Data Agent:

* retrieves insights from stored reports
* provides marketing frameworks

---

# Step 3: Verification

Fact Check Agent:

* reviews collected data
* removes conflicting claims
* prioritizes reliable sources

---

# Step 4: Strategy Generation

Strategy Agent generates a complete report including:

* market overview
* competitor analysis
* audience segmentation
* pricing model
* marketing channels
* launch strategy

---

# Example Output

Sections of the generated report may include:

Market Overview

* growth of AI productivity tools
* student demand for study automation

Competitors

* Notion AI
* Grammarly
* Motion

Target Audience

* university students
* graduate researchers
* productivity-focused learners

Marketing Strategy

* campus ambassador programs
* TikTok marketing campaigns
* student discount pricing
